Documentation Week 2:

This is the link I used to download the ".svg" files. To input these files into Fusion 360, I followed a fairly simply process.

  1. Download .svg files
  2. From here, press enter
  3. Hover over the new shape, and click when hilighted
  4. Find and click "Extrude" in the menu bar, and pull your image into the three dimensional plane
  5. Finally, in the lefthand menu, right click your sketch and save as a DXF file
